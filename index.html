Our Story

Pastaio means "pasta maker" So that's what we do.

We offer fresh, handmade pasta, prepared daily. Pull up a seat and watch our skilled team knead, roll, cut, and shape pasta from scratch.

We've blended the art of serious Italian cooking with a laid-back approach, in a newly designed space in the heart of Tradition Village Square. Come join us for an unforgettable meal alongside a handcrafted cocktail.
